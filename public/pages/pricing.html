Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll still have access until the end of your current billing period.

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No, you can start your 7-day free trial without entering any payment information.

What happens after my free trial ends?

After your trial ends, you can choose to subscribe to continue using all features, or your account will be limited to viewing existing proposals.

Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?

Yes, you can switch between plans at any time. If you switch from monthly to annual, we'll prorate your remaining monthly subscription.

Is there a limit to how many proposals I can create?

Free trial users can create up to 3 proposals. Paid subscribers can create unlimited proposals.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
